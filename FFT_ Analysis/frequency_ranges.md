# Frequency Ranges

This section documents the frequency groupings used during classification testing.

## Classification Groups

### Low Frequency
Used for lower-pitch sounds and bass-style tones.

### Mid Frequency
Used for standard voice-range testing and mid-band audio.

### High Frequency
Used for higher-pitch sounds and sharper frequency peaks.

## Purpose

The classification system compares dominant FFT peaks against predefined frequency regions to determine the output category.
